Life & Times of a Stock Investor

Stock Investing - Pleasure or Pain?

If you have a strong desire to make money through stocks investment, you can Google to reveal countless sites that will pop up with countless information and investment advices. Many believe that after grasping all these information and advices, they will be an expert in stock investing that will open up the Door of Wealth with tons of money flowing through it and voila, another Warren Buffet is born!

Stock investing is fraught with pitfalls. It's an emotional and financial roller coaster ride. At times, it fills us with joy when it lines our pockets handsomely. There are times when it's a painful affair when losses are incurred. Yet there are other times when frustration fills the air when a missed opportunity slips by us. The whole array of emotions are wrapped into one during our stock investing journey.

Often, stock investors trumpet their winnings . Rarely, if at all, do they share their experiences with losses nor the mistakes made. Here, in addition to the know-hows, we paint another side to stocks investing that is based on experience ...... the reality of stock investing.
